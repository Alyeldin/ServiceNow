Step2 (Definning the attributes of this tables,  first schema that will be edited after doing the realtions between tables):

=>users:            (id(PK),  name,  email,  role,  password,  active)
=>customers:        (id(PK), user_name, email, password, phone_number, address)
=>products:         (id(PK), name, category_id(FK), price, description, qty)
=>products_images:  (id(PK), product_id(FK), url)
=>cart:             (id(PK), product_id(FK), customer_id(FK), qty)
=>orders:           (id(PK), order_num, customer_id(FK), total, payment_method_id(FK), shipping_address, status)
=>order_items:      (id(PK), order_id(FK), product_id(FK), qty, price)
=>payment_method:   (id(PK), name, active)
=>categories:       (id(PK), name)
=>setting:          (id(PK), description, user_id(FK))

Step3 (Adding relations between tables):

rel1 :  customers -> products          (m-n) birdge table cart (set product in cart)
rel2 :  products -> products_images    (1-m)
rel3 :  products -> categories         (1-m)
rel4 :  products -> orders             (m-m) birdge table order_items
rel5 :  customers -> orders            (1-m)
rel6 :  payment_method -> orders       (1-1)
