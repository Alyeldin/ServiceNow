SLA (Service Level Agreement):
everything in service-now having an SLA, that got attached to all things done on system , they have types and validations to be created for each and every thing in the system

they are two types:
	Response SLA----------How fast the team will respond to an incident
	Resolution SLA--------How much time will be taken to solve the incident

OLA (Operation Level Agreement)
*SLA Can be attached to a flow like when SLA reach 50% Take an action or perform a notification

Fields of SLA
duration: and its the most important part of SLA that define its deadline
