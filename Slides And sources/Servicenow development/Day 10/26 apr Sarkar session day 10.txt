Advanced reference qualifier

Reference qualifiers in ServiceNow are filters applied to reference fields to restrict the records available for selection, ensuring data accuracy. Located in the dictionary entry under Reference Specifications, they enable, for example, filtering user lookups to only show active employees. Types include simple (conditions), dynamic (script includes), and advanced (JavaScript/encoded queries).

reference specification is in the table dictionary can be managed from there and we can choose which records will be visible
